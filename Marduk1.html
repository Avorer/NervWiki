Marduk Institute

Marduk Institute - is supposedly a secret selection committee that screens all 14-year-old children.

Which could be suitable Evangelion pilots.

However, Ryoji Kaji's research in episode 15 reveals that the Marduk Institute does not actually exist.

This is a fictitious organization consisting of 108 leading companies.

And the Eva pilots are actually handpicked by Nerv.

All potential Eva pilots are then promoted to Class 2-A in Tokyo-3.

Where they will be safe and ready to join Nerv in case of potential danger.
